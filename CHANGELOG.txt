================================================================================
  BUILD INSTRUCTIONS
================================================================================

  Requirements:
    - TDM-GCC 10.3.0 (or any MinGW-w64 g++ with C++17 support)
    - Windows 7 or later

  Build:
    Double-click build.bat
    — or —
    g++ -std=c++17 -O2 -municode -mwindows -DUNICODE -D_UNICODE ^
        filemanager.cpp -o filemanager.exe ^
        -lcomctl32 -lshell32 -lshlwapi -lole32 -loleaut32 -luuid ^
        -lpropsys -luxtheme -lgdi32 -luser32

  To save a version snapshot after a successful build:
    move /y filemanager_new.exe filemanager.exe
    copy filemanager.exe v8.0.exe
    Then update CHANGELOG.txt with the new version entry.
